Understanding the Metrics

Accuracy

Overall correctness of the model's predictions. Higher is better.

Precision

When the model predicts a positive case, how often is it correct? Measures reliability.

Recall (Sensitivity)

How well the model identifies actual positive cases. Critical for medical diagnosis.

Specificity

How well the model identifies actual negative cases. Avoids false alarms.

F1 Score

Harmonic mean of precision and recall. Balances both metrics.

AUC-ROC

Area Under the ROC Curve. Measures overall model performance across all thresholds.
